Top Eco-Friendly Tips for Beginners

Reduce Plastic Use

Start by switching to reusable bags, bottles, and containers. Small changes lead to big environmental impacts.

Save Energy at Home

Use LED lights, unplug unused electronics, and consider energy-efficient appliances to cut down on electricity use.
